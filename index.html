Hi, I'm Hannah!

I am a web designer, application developer, and illustrator from Charlotte, NC. I have a professional background in architecture and management, and a deep love for all things delicious.

Whether your next project is a personal portfolio, an application for your business, or a custom painting for someone you love, I'm your gal!

This is what I'm great at

Web Design

I bring my work to the big and little screen, with a knack for clean presentation and details. From logos to navigation menus, my goal is to highlight your creativity with seamless design and minimal flare.

Web Development

No matter your budget, I can make it work. I believe firmly that every website calls for a different foundation, whether it's a custom structure, or the use of a platform like Wordpress. All you have to worry about is copy and sign-offs.

Illustration

Every engineer must have their craft. When away from the computer screen, I keep my hands moving with a brush or pen in hand. Full self-expression at its finest!

I create things that let me play and that truly express the heart of my clients.
